About Flower Trust

Transforming lives with compassion, dignity, and purpose.

Since 2010

Flower Trust is a registered non-profit rooted in Nagercoil, Tamil Nadu. We're devoted to caring for the elderly, uplifting women and children, and providing essentials for the underprivileged.

24x7 Elder Care

Safe and loving homes for abandoned elderly citizens.

Educational Support

Sponsoring underprivileged children's education.

Food Drives

Distributing food in slums, orphanages & shelters.

Women Empowerment

Skills training, support networks & income programs.
